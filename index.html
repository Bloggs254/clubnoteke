Our Story

More Than Just a Car Club. It's a Family.

Founded in 2021, CLUBnoteKE started with a small group of passionate Nissan Note owners who wanted to share their experiences and help each other with car maintenance in Kenya.

Today, we have grown into a vibrant community that celebrates the versatility and reliability of the Nissan Note through road trips, charity drives, and technical workshops.

  • Expert mechanical advice and trusted garages.
  • Monthly meetups and scenic road trips across Kenya.
  • Exclusive discounts on spare parts and servicing.
