Our Story

The story behind Kenya's fastest growing car community.

Our Mission

Driving Together, Growing Together

To foster a supportive and passionate community of Nissan Note owners in Kenya by sharing knowledge, organizing engaging events, and giving back to society through concerted charitable efforts.

Our Vision

The Ultimate Car Family

To be recognized as the premier car club in East Africa, known not just for our vehicles, but for the strength, unity, and positive impact of our members.

Our Journey

April 2021

The Beginning

Founded by a group of 5 friends in Nairobi who wanted to share maintenance costs and DIY tips.

December 2022

First Major Convoy

Organized a 50-car convoy to Naivasha, officially establishing our presence on Kenyan roads.

August 2024

National Registration

Officially registered as a club in Kenya, crossing the 800 active member milestone.

Today

A Thriving Community

Hosting monthly events, maintaining a 24/7 support forum, and engaging in continuous charity work.
