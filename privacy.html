Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

1. Introduction

At CLUBnoteKE, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our members. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or join our community.

2. Information We Collect

When you apply for membership or contact us, we may collect the following information:

  • Full Name and Contact Details (Email, Phone Number)
  • Vehicle Information (Model, Year, Customizations)
  • Location (City/Region) for coordinating regional meets
  • Transaction details for membership verification

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for club-related purposes, including:

  • Verifying membership and processing applications via Formspree
  • Providing access to the private WhatsApp group and Online Forum
  • Sending event invitations and club newsletters
  • Maintaining the Member Dashboard experience

4. Data Security

We implement a multi-layered security approach, including Content Security Policy (CSP) and Subresource Integrity (SRI) on our website. Member name data for the dashboard is stored locally in your browser (localStorage) to ensure privacy from unauthorized server-side access during this phase of our platform.

5. Third-Party Services

We share data only with trusted partners essential for club operations:

  • Formspree: For processing membership application forms securely.
  • WhatsApp: For facilitating community communication (subject to WhatsApp's own privacy terms).

6. Your Rights

Members have the right to request a copy of their stored data or request the deletion of their records at any time by contacting our Admin team through the official channels listed on our Contact page.
