Advanced Technologies

The engineering that powers the Note's efficiency.

Hybrid Evolution

e-POWER System

A series-hybrid system where the wheels are 100% driven by an electric motor. The gasoline engine (HR12) acts purely as a generator to charge the battery.

  • E12 Note: 109HP / 254Nm torque.
  • E13 Note: 116HP / 280Nm torque.
  • e-Pedal: Allows starting, accelerating, and decelerating to a stop using only the accelerator.
Supercharged

DIG-S (HR12DDR)

Direct Injection Gasoline Supercharger. This engine uses the Miller Cycle and an on-demand supercharger to achieve 1.5L power with 1.0L economy.

  • Output: 98 PS / 142 Nm.
  • Efficiency: High compression ratio (12:1+) for maximum thermal efficiency.
  • Response: Instant torque from the Eaton supercharger.
Efficiency Badge

Pure Drive

Nissan's designation for vehicles that exceed emissions standards. It incorporates a suite of eco-technologies working in harmony.

  • Idling Stop: Automatically shuts off the engine at traffic lights.
  • Xtronic CVT: Optimized for smoothness and fuel conservation.
  • Eco Mode: Remaps throttle and CVT for maximum distance per liter.

  • All Minor items

Never skip ATF oil change on CVT models. This is the single most important service item for your import.

Parts Availability

Service parts are affordable and ubiquitous in KE. Body parts are becoming common for E12. Warning: E11 and E12 parts are NOT interchangeable. Always verify chassis code NE11/E11 vs E12/NE12 before buying.

Watch Out For

  • CVT Shudder: Common on high-mileage imports. Fixed by fresh ATF.
  • DIG-S Oil: Requires premium supercharger oil.
